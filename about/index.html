Who We Are

About Envert Canada

We generate clean energy from several types of waste — pioneering Cold Catalytic Conversion in North America.

Our Mission

We promote a unique,
cost-effective technology

We promote a unique and very cost-effective industrial process based on the Cold Catalytic Conversion technique, that allows the production of light synthetic fuel oil with a high calorific value and very low carbon footprint. The final product has several uses and is highly valued by consumers.

Mission: Energizing waste, Powering a cleaner industry.

Value: Commitment to sustainability and fairness.

Vision: Protecting Earth, empowering People.

Range of Waste Recycling

We can process all these waste types

  • Refused Derived Fuel (RDF)
  • All types of plastic, except PVC
  • Rubber and used tires
  • Wood residues
  • Industrial waste and household waste
  • Lightweight and polymeric packaging
  • Textile waste
  • Mixtures of all the above waste types
Our Structure

Who We Are & What We Do

EnVert — The Holding

EnVert is a holding company owned by Thermo Transcal Inc. and BIOCON GmbH / ESA AG — combining world-class Canadian thermal engineering with proven German waste-to-fuel conversion expertise.

Thermo Transcal Inc.

Specialized in thermal engineering. Developed expertise in the design and manufacture of innovative heat exchangers — reliable and very safe — with a portfolio of more than 2,000 installations worldwide. Controlling shareholder of the holding.

BIOCON GmbH & ESA AG

German companies specializing in the development and manufacture of equipment that recovers energy from waste. Three turnkey plants operating in Hannover and München (Germany), and Albacete (Spain).

The Technology

Based on Cold Catalytic Conversion — a successful industrial scale-up converting several types of waste into fuels. Our solution is the first implemented in North America, providing reliable, high-profitability, and minimum-maintenance energy production.
