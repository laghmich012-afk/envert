About Envert Canada

We generate clean energy from several types of waste — pioneering Cold Catalytic Conversion in North America since 2014.

Our Mission

A unique, cost-effective technology

We promote a unique and very cost-effective industrial process based on the Cold Catalytic Conversion technique, producing light synthetic fuel oil with a high calorific value and a very low carbon footprint. The final product has several uses and is highly valued across industries.

Our Structure

Who We Are & What We Do

EnVert is a holding company that combines world-class Canadian thermal engineering with proven German waste-to-fuel expertise — resulting in the first CCC plant implementation in North America.

EnVert — The Holding

EnVert Canada Inc. is the holding entity owned by Thermo Transcal Inc. and BIOCON GmbH / ESA AG. We bring together complementary expertise in thermal engineering and waste-to-energy conversion to deliver a truly innovative solution for the North American market.

Thermo Transcal Inc.

Specialized in thermal engineering with expertise in the design and manufacture of innovative, reliable heat exchangers. A portfolio of more than 2,000 installations worldwide makes Thermo Transcal the controlling shareholder and operational backbone of the holding.

BIOCON GmbH & ESA AG

German companies specializing in the development and manufacture of equipment that recovers energy from waste. Three turnkey CCC plants are currently operating in Hannover and München (Germany) and Albacete (Spain) — proving the technology at industrial scale.

The Technology Partnership

By combining Thermo Transcal's heat exchange expertise with BIOCON's CCC process and Prof. Schraufstetter's patents, Envert has created an industrially scalable technology converting several types of waste into fuels using the same process — a world first at this scale in North America.
