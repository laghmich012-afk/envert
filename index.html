What We Produce

From every tonne of waste,
multiple energy outputs

Waste Recycling

RDF, plastics, organics, textiles, used tires, and wood — all processed in a single reactor with no major adjustments.

Synthetic Fuel

High-quality light fuel refined into Naphtha, Gasoline, Kerosene, or Diesel — direct replacement for fossil fuels.

Heat

Cogeneration and sale of heat for neighboring industrial plants via our efficient heat exchangers.

Electricity

Generated electricity sold to the public grid, for self-consumption, or supplied directly to neighboring plants.

Why our technology is different

01

Efficiency

1,000 kg of waste converts to up to 850 litres of high-quality fuel. Average conversion rate exceeds 70%.

02

Flexibility

A single installation processes multiple types of waste — recyclable and non-recyclable — without major changes.

03

Compactness

The reactor fits in a 40-ft container and can be transported, relocated, or expanded in parallel modules.

04

Reliability

Remote monitoring via internet from any device. Runs 24 hours a day with minimal human intervention.

05

Clean Environment

Closed reactor at low temperatures. No greenhouse gases emitted. No risk of leakage, pollution, or explosion.

06

Durability

20–25 year lifecycle. Maintenance only twice per year — one of the lowest total-cost solutions available.
