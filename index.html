What We Produce

From every tonne of waste,
multiple energy outputs

Waste Recycling

RDF, plastics, organics, textiles, used tires, and wood — all processed in a single reactor.

Synthetic Fuel

High-quality light fuel with a very high calorific value — refined into Naphtha, Gasoline, Kerosene, or Diesel.

Heat

Cogeneration and sale of heat for neighboring plants via our heat exchangers.

Electricity

Generated electricity sold to the grid, for self-consumption, or to neighboring industrial plants.

Why our
technology is different

Full Technology →
01

Efficiency

1,000 kg of waste converts to up to 850 litres of high-quality fuel. Average conversion rate exceeds 70%.

02

Flexibility

A single installation processes multiple types of waste — recyclable and non-recyclable — without major changes.

03

Compactness

The reactor fits in a 40-ft container and can be transported, relocated, or expanded in parallel modules.

04

Reliability

Remote monitoring via internet from any device. Runs 24 hours a day with minimal human intervention.

05

Clean Environment

Closed reactor operating at low temperatures. No greenhouse gases emitted. No risk of leakage, pollution, or explosion.

06

Durability

20–25 year lifecycle. Very little maintenance required — only twice per year — keeping operating costs extremely low.
