The People Behind Envert

Our Leadership Team

Engineers, executives and inventors united by a common mission — turning the world's waste problem into a clean energy opportunity.

"At EnVert, we work as a team and combine our talents to develop an innovative technology that has solved the problem of recycling waste in a cost-effective, sustainable way."

Founder & CEO
Tarik Laghmich
CEO — Envert Inc. & Thermo Transcal Inc.
MSc Electrical Engineering · GR Business Management

Co-founder of both Envert Canada and Thermo Transcal Inc., Tarik brings deep expertise in thermal engineering, project management, and industrial innovation. He holds several internationally registered PCT patents and has led the deployment of tubular heat exchanger technologies in over 2,000 installations worldwide.

  • Organisations & Projects Management
  • Thermal engineering and energy efficiency
  • Multiple patents registered in the international PCT system
  • Co-Founder & CEO, Thermo Transcal Inc. — tubular heat exchangers

Inventor & CTO
Prof. Wilfried Schraufstetter
Inventor of CCC Technology · CTO, Biocon GmbH
Mechanical Engineer · Master Electrician · 20+ Patents

Professor Schraufstetter is the inventor of Cold Catalytic Conversion technology and the scientific force behind Envert's core process. Since the early 1980s he has developed biogas and waste-to-energy systems, successfully scaling CCC technology to industrial production across three operating plants in Europe.

  • Inventor of the Cold Catalytic Conversion (CCC) process
  • Developing waste-to-energy systems since the early 1980s
  • Recovering fuels from plastics since 1995 — industrial success
  • 3 CCC plants scaled to full industrial operation
  • 20+ patents in industrial process engineering
