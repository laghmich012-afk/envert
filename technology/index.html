Our Technology

A patented, closed-loop industrial process converting waste into clean fuel — with no greenhouse gas emissions and over 70% conversion efficiency.

The CCC Reactor

Nothing left behind

Plastic waste is a massive global problem. Burning or landfilling are not real alternatives — and material recycling fails for mixed and composite materials. Envert, in partnership with Biocon Energy GmbH, offers an environmentally friendly, reliable and efficient solution based on the Cold Catalytic Conversion technique.

The CCC reactor handles 10–20 tonnes of waste per day, runs 24 hours continuously, and processes different types of waste without major changes. Operating at approximately 1 bar — no pressure danger, no emissions, fully closed system. The process basis has been known for over 40 years through catalytic conversion of fossil fuel products; today it is an industrially sustainable factory.

The Process

How the CCC reactor works

Waste Input

Material grain-sized 3–15mm, dried and granulated, fed to an extruder and compressed. Plastics, rubber, RDF, mixed waste.

CCC Conversion

Cold catalytic conversion at low temperature (~1 bar). Continuous 24h operation. Oil vapors evaporate at the reactor outlet.

Condensation

Resulting substances condensed, purified and collected in separate tanks by hydrocarbon chain length for further use.

Energy Output

Fuel, electricity (via CHP cogeneration), and heat — all sold to industry, neighbouring plants, or the power grid.

Technical Advantages

Six reasons to choose CCC technology

01

Efficiency

Consumes little energy and offers a very high conversion rate. On average, conversion of waste to fuels exceeds 70%. 1,000 kg waste → up to 850 litres of high-quality fuel.

02

Flexibility

Modular system — a single installation processes several types of waste, recyclable and non-recyclable. The reactor adjusts easily to the quantity of raw materials available.

03

Compactness

Compact and removable — transportable site to site in a 40-ft container. Upgrades can be added in parallel or superimposed modules as required.

04

Reliability

Monitoring via a remote platform by internet from any device. Predictive maintenance sensors minimize downtime. Runs 24 hours per day.

05

Environment

Little risk of leakage, pollution or explosion. A closed reactor operating at low temperatures — does not emit greenhouse gases. Zero liquid waste discharge.

06

Durability

Long life cycle of 20 to 25 years. Maintenance only twice per year — one of the lowest total-cost of ownership solutions available on the market.
